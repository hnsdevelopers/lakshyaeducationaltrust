Ways to Get Involved

Your support can make a real difference in the lives of underprivileged youth.

Volunteer With Us

Join our team of dedicated volunteers. Teach English, help with library management, or assist in our campaigns.

Donate Books & Resources

We accept donations of new/used books (competitive exam materials, novels, children's books), stationery, and furniture.

Become a Mentor

Professionals and educators can mentor our youth in career guidance, spoken English, or digital literacy.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer?

Fill out the contact form with subject "Volunteer" and we'll reach out with opportunities.

Do you accept monetary donations?

Lakshya operates without external funding. However, for major initiatives, we can provide details upon inquiry.

How do I reach the office?

We are located in Rajvir Colony, East Delhi. Nearest metro: IP Extension (Blue Line), then take an auto-rickshaw.
